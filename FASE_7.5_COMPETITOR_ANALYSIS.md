## ConcertRide's Competitive Edges

1. **Zero Commission**: Undercuts BlaBlaCar by 12–13 percentage points
2. **Vertical Specialization**: Owns festival category vs. generalist platforms
3. **Programmatic Content Scale**: 600+ festival–city combinations (routes) = massive keyword coverage vs. Caroster's 20 manually-curated events
4. **Transport Expertise**: Blog targets specific pain points (night returns, carbon footprint, "alternativas a BlaBlaCar")
5. **Local Geo-SEO**: Per-festival FAQs + venue data + region pages = rich, contextual content
